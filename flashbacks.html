flashbacks
by Stephanie Valencia

Thinking back to when I was in fourth grade

with flashbacks of gut-wrenching sorrow,

I can't help but feel tears start to cascade,


hoping that day was all a night terror.



Early that morning I rushed to the scene


saw you laying motionless in the doorway,

but as I ran to you... I was stopped! Unforseen,

some officers reluctantly shooed me away.



I waited with family in a small room.

We stayed hopeful, but soon were struck with heartache

when the doctor came in and told us the news,

that the wound in your heart could't keep you awake.


You look so at ease with your hands near your chest,

as we unwillingly put you to rest.
