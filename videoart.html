Video Art | Tell A Story Without Telling The Story




In this short video, I hoped to convey a lighthearted portrayal between reality and fantasy in terms of heartbreak (reading in this case). To make the work more dynamic, I incorporated different camera angles along with video transitions to help the entire video have fluidity. I also think that with the addition of audio it helped to have both the lighthearted and melancholic mood I was going for. It was my first time editing on premier, and I must admit I ran into complications here and there. Nonetheless, I am very satisfied with the final product. I went with something more simple, but I believe it works well. Hope it causes you to chuckle a bit! Enjoy!
